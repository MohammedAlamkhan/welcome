Overview

Bandra Bandstand is a popular seaside promenade located in Bandra, Mumbai. Known for its scenic views, it is a favorite spot for joggers, walkers, and those seeking a peaceful place to relax by the Arabian Sea.

Location

Bandra West, Mumbai, Maharashtra 400050, India

History

Bandra Bandstand has been a popular hangout spot for decades. Historically, it was known for its bandstand concerts, where bands would perform for the public. Today, it is more famous for its celebrity residences and beautiful sunset views.

Nearby Attractions

Nearby attractions include the Bandra Fort (Castella de Aguada), Mount Mary Church, and the bustling Bandra-Worli Sea Link. The area is also known for its trendy cafes and restaurants, making it a great spot for a day out.

Visitor Information

Bandra Bandstand is open to the public all day and night. It is a free attraction, making it accessible to everyone. The best time to visit is during the early morning or late afternoon to avoid the heat and enjoy the pleasant sea breeze.
