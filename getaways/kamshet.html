Overview

Kamshet is a scenic hill station located in the Pune district of Maharashtra. Known for its picturesque landscapes and adventure sports, it is a popular destination for paragliding enthusiasts. The region offers stunning views of the Western Ghats and is surrounded by lush greenery, making it an ideal spot for outdoor activities and relaxation.

Location

Kamshet, Pune District, Maharashtra 411001, India

Attractions

Kamshet is renowned for its adventure sports, particularly paragliding, which offers spectacular aerial views of the lush green hills and valleys. Other attractions include the beautiful Bedse Caves, the serene Pavana Lake, and the historic Kondeshwar Temple. The area's natural beauty and tranquility make it a popular destination for both adventure seekers and nature lovers.

Activities

In Kamshet, visitors can engage in various activities such as paragliding, trekking, and camping. The paragliding experience offers a unique way to enjoy the stunning landscape from above. Trekking trails around the hills and camping near the lakes provide opportunities for exploration and adventure. Additionally, visitors can enjoy boating on Pavana Lake and explore the ancient Bedse Caves.

Visitor Information

Kamshet is accessible by road from Pune and Mumbai, with the drive offering picturesque views. The best time to visit is from October to March when the weather is pleasant. There are various accommodation options ranging from budget lodges to luxury resorts. Visitors should carry comfortable clothing and footwear for outdoor activities and check weather conditions before traveling.
