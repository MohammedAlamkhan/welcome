Overview

Alibaug is a picturesque coastal town located in the Raigad district of Maharashtra, known for its beautiful beaches, historical forts, and lush greenery. It is a popular weekend getaway from Mumbai, offering a serene escape with its sandy shores and tranquil surroundings. The town features several charming beaches and a variety of water sports, making it a favorite destination for relaxation and adventure.

Location

Alibaug, Raigad District, Maharashtra 402201, India

Attractions

Alibaug is renowned for its beautiful beaches such as Alibaug Beach, Nagaon Beach, and Kashid Beach. Visitors can explore historical forts like the Kolaba Fort and the Murud-Janjira Fort. The town also offers lush gardens, scenic views, and opportunities for water sports such as jet skiing and parasailing.

Activities

Visitors to Alibaug can indulge in various activities including beach outings, exploring historical forts, and enjoying water sports. The town is ideal for relaxing by the sea, trying local seafood, and experiencing the vibrant local culture. Beachside shacks and markets offer a taste of local cuisine and shopping options.

Visitor Information

Alibaug is accessible by ferry from Mumbai or by road. It is advisable to visit during the cooler months from October to March. Accommodations range from luxury resorts to budget hotels. Local transportation includes auto-rickshaws and taxis. Visitors should be mindful of the weather and plan their travel accordingly.
