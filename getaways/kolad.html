Overview

Kolad is a charming village in Raigad district, Maharashtra, known for its picturesque landscapes and adventure activities. It is nestled along the banks of the Kundalika River and is famous for its white-water rafting experiences. Kolad offers a perfect escape for nature lovers and adventure enthusiasts seeking tranquility and excitement.

Location

Kolad, Raigad District, Maharashtra 402304, India

Attractions

Kolad is renowned for its thrilling white-water rafting on the Kundalika River, which is a major draw for adventure seekers. Other attractions include the scenic Tamhini Ghat, the majestic Kuda Caves, and the tranquil Pali Village. The lush green landscapes and serene riverside views offer a perfect setting for relaxation and exploration.

Activities

Kolad offers a range of activities including white-water rafting, trekking, and camping. Visitors can enjoy river rafting on the Kundalika River, explore the picturesque Tamhini Ghat, and visit the historic Kuda Caves. The area is also great for nature walks, picnicking, and bird watching.

Visitor Information

Kolad is easily accessible by road from Mumbai and Pune. The best time to visit is during the monsoon season from June to September for the best rafting experience. There are various accommodation options ranging from budget lodges to luxury resorts. Visitors should carry appropriate clothing for adventure activities and check weather conditions before planning their trip.
