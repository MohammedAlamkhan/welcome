Overview

Pawna is a picturesque lake located near Lonavala in Maharashtra. Known for its serene environment and stunning views of the surrounding hills, it is a popular destination for camping, picnicking, and outdoor activities. The lake is an ideal spot for a tranquil getaway amidst nature, offering beautiful sunset views and opportunities for recreational activities.

Location

Pawna Lake, Lonavala, Pune District, Maharashtra 410401, India

Attractions

The primary attraction at Pawna is the serene lake itself, which offers stunning views and a peaceful ambiance. Visitors can enjoy boating, camping by the lakeside, and picnicking with family and friends. The surrounding hills and forests add to the natural beauty of the area, making it a popular spot for nature lovers and adventure enthusiasts. The lake is also a great place to experience the local flora and fauna.

Activities

At Pawna Lake, visitors can engage in various outdoor activities including camping, boating, and trekking. Camping by the lake is a popular activity, providing an opportunity to enjoy the tranquility of nature and starry nights. Boating on the lake offers a serene experience with scenic views of the surrounding hills. There are also several trekking trails around the lake that provide excellent viewpoints and opportunities to explore the natural landscape.

Visitor Information

Pawna Lake is easily accessible by road from Pune and Mumbai. The best time to visit is from October to March when the weather is pleasant. There are various camping sites and accommodations available around the lake. Visitors should carry comfortable clothing, sunscreen, and mosquito repellent.
