Overview

Phoenix Marketcity is one of Mumbai's largest shopping malls, offering a vast array of retail stores, dining options, and entertainment facilities. With a perfect blend of high-end brands, casual shopping, and entertainment experiences, it's a popular destination for families and shopping enthusiasts alike.

Location

Phoenix Marketcity, LBS Road, Kurla West, Mumbai, Maharashtra 400070, India

Shopping

Phoenix Marketcity is home to a wide range of retail outlets, from global luxury brands to popular high-street stores. Visitors can find everything from fashion and accessories to electronics and home decor, making it a one-stop destination for all shopping needs.

Dining

The mall offers an extensive selection of dining options, including fine dining restaurants, fast food joints, and cozy cafes. Whether you're in the mood for international cuisine or local flavors, Phoenix Marketcity has something to satisfy every palate.

Entertainment

Phoenix Marketcity features a multiplex cinema, gaming zones, and regular events, ensuring entertainment for visitors of all ages. From movie screenings to live performances, there's always something happening to keep the excitement alive.

Visitor Information

Phoenix Marketcity is open daily, with shopping and dining options available from 10:00 AM to 10:00 PM. The mall offers ample parking and is easily accessible by public transport. For the latest updates on events and promotions, visitors can check the mall's official website and social media channels.
