Overview

The Phoenix Palladium Mall is a high-end shopping and entertainment destination located in Lower Parel, Mumbai. It is known for its luxury retail stores, fine dining restaurants, and state-of-the-art entertainment facilities. The mall features a contemporary design with upscale interiors and offers a premium shopping experience in the heart of the city.

Location

Phoenix Palladium Mall, Lower Parel, Mumbai, Maharashtra 400013, India

Shopping

The Phoenix Palladium Mall hosts a variety of luxury and high-end brands, including international designers and premium Indian labels. Shoppers can find the latest fashion trends, accessories, and exclusive products across multiple floors. The mall also features specialty stores offering gourmet food, cosmetics, and electronics.

Dining

The mall offers a diverse range of dining options, from fine dining restaurants to casual eateries. Guests can enjoy a selection of cuisines, including Indian, Continental, and Asian dishes. Some of the renowned dining establishments include gourmet burger joints, sushi bars, and upscale cafes.

Entertainment

The Phoenix Palladium Mall features state-of-the-art entertainment facilities, including a multi-screen cinema complex and a vibrant food court. Visitors can catch the latest movies in comfort, or enjoy live performances and events that are regularly hosted at the mall.

Visitor Information

The Phoenix Palladium Mall is open seven days a week, with timings from 11:00 AM to 11:00 PM. Parking facilities are available, and the mall is well-connected by public transportation. For the latest updates on events, store listings, and dining options, visitors can check the mall's official website or social media pages.
