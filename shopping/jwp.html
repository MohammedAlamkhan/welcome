Overview

Jio World Plaza is a modern, upscale shopping and entertainment destination located in Bandra Kurla Complex, Mumbai. Known for its sleek design and contemporary architecture, the plaza features a diverse range of high-end retail stores, luxury boutiques, and fine dining restaurants. The complex also includes a state-of-the-art cinema and a spacious, well-designed public plaza.

Location

Jio World Plaza, Bandra Kurla Complex, Bandra East, Mumbai, Maharashtra 400051, India

Shopping

Jio World Plaza boasts a range of premium and luxury brands, offering a shopping experience that caters to high-end tastes. Visitors can explore exclusive fashion boutiques, high-tech electronics stores, and artisanal shops featuring unique and bespoke products.

Dining

The plaza features an impressive selection of dining options, from gourmet restaurants and chic cafes to casual eateries. Guests can enjoy a diverse range of cuisines, including Indian, Continental, and Asian dishes, all within a vibrant and stylish setting.

Entertainment

Entertainment at Jio World Plaza includes a modern cinema complex showcasing the latest films in high-definition. The plaza also hosts various events, including live performances, cultural shows, and exhibitions, offering a dynamic and engaging experience for visitors.

Visitor Information

Jio World Plaza is open daily, with stores and dining options operating from 10:00 AM to 10:00 PM. The complex offers ample parking facilities and is easily accessible by public transportation. For the latest updates on events and promotions, visitors can check the plaza's official website and social media channels.
