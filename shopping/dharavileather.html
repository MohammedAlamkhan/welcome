Overview

Dharavi Leather Market is a bustling hub in Mumbai known for its wide range of high-quality leather goods at affordable prices. The market is famous for its locally crafted leather products, including bags, belts, wallets, and jackets, making it a must-visit for those seeking authentic and budget-friendly leather items.

Location

Dharavi Leather Market, Dharavi, Mumbai, Maharashtra 400017, India

Attractions

Besides the extensive selection of leather goods, Dharavi Leather Market is located within the larger Dharavi area, which is known for its vibrant community of artisans and craftsmen. Visitors can explore the nearby workshops where skilled artisans create leather goods, offering a glimpse into the traditional leather-making process.

Activities

Shopping for leather products is the main activity at Dharavi Leather Market, where visitors can find unique and custom-made items. The market offers opportunities to interact with local artisans, learn about their craft, and even commission personalized leather goods. Bargaining is common, so shoppers can enjoy negotiating for the best deals.

Visitor Information

Dharavi Leather Market is open daily, with the best time to visit being in the morning when the market is less crowded. The market is accessible by public transportation, including local trains, buses, and taxis. Visitors are advised to wear comfortable shoes and be prepared for a lively, bustling environment.
