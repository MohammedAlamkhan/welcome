Overview

Nexus Seawoods is a premier shopping and entertainment destination located in Seawoods, Navi Mumbai. The mall features a modern design with a spacious layout and a wide range of shopping options, from high-street fashion to luxury brands. In addition to its diverse retail offerings, Nexus Seawoods includes a variety of dining options and an entertainment zone.

Location

Nexus Seawoods, Seawoods West, Navi Mumbai, Maharashtra 400706, India

Shopping

Nexus Seawoods offers an extensive range of shopping experiences, featuring both national and international brands. From fashion and electronics to home decor and beauty products, shoppers can find a wide variety of items under one roof.

Dining

The mall's dining options include a mix of casual eateries, fine dining restaurants, and fast food outlets. Visitors can enjoy diverse cuisines, from local delicacies to international dishes, all within a vibrant and comfortable setting.

Entertainment

Nexus Seawoods features a modern multiplex cinema with the latest films and a state-of-the-art sound system. The entertainment zone also hosts various events and activities, making it a popular spot for family outings and social gatherings.

Visitor Information

The mall is open daily from 10:00 AM to 10:00 PM. Ample parking is available, and Nexus Seawoods is easily accessible by public transport. For updates on events, promotions, and store information, visitors can check the mall's official website and social media channels.
