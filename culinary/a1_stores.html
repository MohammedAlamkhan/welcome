Location

A1 Bakery, St. John Baptist Road, Bandra West, Mumbai, Maharashtra 400050, India

Ambience

A1 Bakery is a beloved institution in Bandra, known for its iconic chicken puffs and a wide array of other baked delights. With its old-world charm and simple setup, the bakery has a nostalgic feel that keeps locals and visitors coming back for more. The bustling yet cozy atmosphere makes it a perfect spot to enjoy freshly baked goods in the heart of Bandra.
