Aram Vadapao, Opposite CST Station, Mumbai, Maharashtra 400001, India

Ambience

Aram Vadapao is a legendary spot in Mumbai, known for its simple yet iconic Vada Pav. The ambiance is unpretentious, offering a quick and casual dining experience. The hustle and bustle around CST adds to the charm, making it a favorite for locals and tourists alike.
