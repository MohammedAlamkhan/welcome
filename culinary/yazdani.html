Ambience

Yazdani Bakery is one of Mumbai's oldest and most beloved bakeries, steeped in history and tradition. The quaint, old-world charm of the bakery, with its wooden interiors and vintage decor, offers a nostalgic atmosphere that takes you back in time. It’s the perfect place to enjoy freshly baked bread, Irani tea, and a slice of Mumbai’s rich culinary heritage.
