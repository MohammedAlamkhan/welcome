Explore a wide variety of street food offerings from various stalls, featuring everything from chaat, vada pav, pav bhaji, kebabs, dosas, to refreshing sugarcane juice and more.

Location

Khau Galli, Multiple Locations (Ghatkopar, Zaveri Bazaar, Tardeo), Mumbai, Maharashtra, India

Ambience

Khau Galli, literally translating to "food street," is a vibrant and bustling hub of street food in Mumbai. Known for its lively atmosphere, the aroma of delicious food fills the air as you walk through the lanes. From locals grabbing a quick bite to food enthusiasts exploring diverse flavors, Khau Galli offers a quintessential Mumbai street food experience, with something for everyone.
