Location

Mysore Cafe, D N Road, King's Circle, Matunga East, Mumbai, Maharashtra 400019, India

Ambience

Mysore Cafe is a cherished spot in Matunga, known for its authentic South Indian cuisine. The cafe's simple and humble setting provides a warm and inviting atmosphere. It's a favorite among locals who come for the delicious dosas, idlis, and filter coffee. The traditional decor and unpretentious vibe make it a perfect place for a peaceful meal.
