Alibaug

An iconic arch monument built in the early 20th century, standing majestically on the waterfront in South Mumbai.

Combo of Hindu and Muslim architecture. 🕌🏛️

Lonavala

A 3.6-kilometer-long boulevard in South Mumbai, offering stunning views of the Arabian Sea and nicknamed the "Queen's Necklace".

Marine Drive forms a perfect 'C' shape. 🌊🔄

Kolad

A historic railway station and UNESCO World Heritage Site known for its Victorian Gothic Revival architecture.

Completed in 1887, Gothic Revival style. 🏰🕰️

kamshet

Ancient cave temples dedicated to Lord Shiva, located on Elephanta Island in Mumbai Harbour.

UNESCO World Heritage Site. 🌐🏅

Pawna

A floating mosque and tomb located 500 meters off the coast, connected to the city by a narrow causeway.

Visited by people of all faiths. 🙏🌍
